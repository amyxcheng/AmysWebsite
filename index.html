About Me

Hi, I'm Amy; welcome to my website! I am currently a second year double degree student studying Computer Science at the University of Waterloo, and Business Administration at Wilfrid Laurier University. I am an eager learner and lover of creating new things.
I originally intended to create this website and use it as a platform for showcasing some of the projects I've worked on. However, I would also like to use this site as a place for me to express myself beyond the projects and boasting of relevant work experiences in attempts to attract job recruiters. Whether it is through words, pictures, art, or code, my goal is to be able to create a sort of public, creative diary (such as with social media, but without the FOMO feeling that comes with scrolling through endless amounts of perfectly edited photos posted by friends and strangers alike).
This website was created using HTML/CSS and Javascript after taking introductory online courses for those languages. It is hosted through my Github.
